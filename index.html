Nine Times the Cheer

Broadway shows have the ability to push us to the edge of our emotional spectrum. They fill our hearts with immeasurable joy one moment, then break them into a million pieces the next. And we love it.

The Peace Center’s 2017-2018 Broadway Season is crafted with nine unforgettable shows designed to make you laugh, cry and, at times, stand and cheer. Nine incomparable stories connecting with you in nine unique ways. Your season ticket gives you access to all of it: the music, the magic, the costumes and choreography, the performances and pageantry… nine times over!

And as if that weren’t enough, it puts you first in line for next season, when we bring you the story of America then, as told by America now… Hamilton!

Direct from the lights of Broadway to the heart of Greenville, the Peace Center is proud to present our 2017-2018 Broadway Season!
